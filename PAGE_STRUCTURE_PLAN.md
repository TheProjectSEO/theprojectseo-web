# TheProjectSEO - Comprehensive Page Structure Plan

## Service Pages Structure

### 1. AEO Services (/services/aeo)
- **Focus**: Advanced Enterprise Optimization
- **Target**: Large enterprises and complex websites
- **Content**: Technical audits, enterprise-scale implementations, multi-site management
- **Key Features**: Advanced technical SEO, international SEO, large-scale content optimization

### 2. SEO Services (/services/seo)
- **Focus**: Core SEO optimization services
- **Target**: All business sizes
- **Content**: Keyword research, on-page optimization, link building, technical SEO
- **Key Features**: Rankings improvement, organic traffic growth, competitor analysis

### 3. Content Services (/services/content)
- **Focus**: Content creation and optimization
- **Target**: Businesses needing content strategy
- **Content**: Content audits, creation, optimization, strategy development
- **Key Features**: Content gap analysis, topic clusters, content performance tracking

### 4. Web Services (/services/web)
- **Focus**: Website development and optimization
- **Target**: Businesses needing web development with SEO integration
- **Content**: SEO-optimized websites, technical implementations, performance optimization
- **Key Features**: Core Web Vitals, mobile optimization, site architecture

## Page Template Structure

Each page will include:
1. **Hero Section**: Compelling headline, value proposition, CTA
2. **Services/Solutions**: Specific offerings for that category
3. **Benefits**: Why choose us for this specific need
4. **Case Studies/Results**: Industry/location-specific examples
5. **Process**: How we deliver results
6. **Testimonials**: Relevant social proof
7. **FAQ**: Common questions for that segment
8. **CTA Section**: Next steps and contact

## Content Strategy

### Service Pages
- Focus on specific service benefits and deliverables
- Include pricing indicators and service packages
- Showcase relevant tools and methodologies
- Feature service-specific case studies

### Industry Pages
- Address industry-specific challenges and regulations
- Include relevant keywords and terminology
- Showcase industry expertise and results
- Feature industry-specific case studies and testimonials

### Location Pages
- Address local market knowledge and cultural understanding
- Include local SEO strategies and regulations
- Showcase regional success stories
- Feature local business directories and citations

## SEO Optimization

### URL Structure
- Clean, descriptive URLs
- Proper use of hyphens and lowercase
- Logical hierarchy and breadcrumbs

### Meta Tags
- Unique title tags for each page
- Compelling meta descriptions
- Proper heading structure (H1, H2, H3)

### Internal Linking
- Strategic cross-linking between related pages
- Category and subcategory navigation
- Related content suggestions

### Schema Markup
- Service schema for service pages
- LocalBusiness schema for location pages
- Organization schema for company pages
- FAQ schema where applicable
